My Specialities

Anxiety

One of my main focuses I help manage is with Anxiety. With over 40 million US adults and children facing overwhelming Anxiety it has become something that affects our friends, our families, and even many of us. We don't have to accept defeat!

Depression

With over 280 million individuals diagnosed with Depression we need the help we deserve, especially in these ever changing, and difficult times.

Substance Abuse

Substance Abuse is a difficult subject for many. It can make an amazing parent or family member have the behavior of a poor parental figure. I'd love to understand your situations and be able to help find healthier coping methods!

Childhood Trauma

As difficult as it is to open up about this, or any other trauma we may have faced, it's part of accepting the past and creating the future. I won't ever judge your situation or how you were brought up. I truly want you to be happy in your skin and happy with who you are today!
